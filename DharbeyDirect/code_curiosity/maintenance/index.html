CodeCuriosity, a platform that both promotes and rewards work on open source software, is being reborn.

We can't give you a specific timeline yet, but we are working hard on improving, revamping, and retooling the platform to better support and reward open source development in 2021 and beyond.

What is CodeCuriosity?

CodeCuriosity is a platform to support and to reward open source contributions. For the individual, it tracks, recognizes, and rewards your work on open source with tangible benefits. For companies and organizations, it provides a platform for them to reward work in areas that they are interested in, and it enables them to access valuable metrics on where and how their incentives are being applied.

Who should use CodeCuriosity?

Everyone who contributes to open source. It's not only fun, but you're also making a difference!

How does CodeCuriosity benefit me?

You earn points for contributions. You can redeem points for rewards. You're contributing back to the community!

How does it work?

Just keep on contributing and earn points. Redeem points and buy something from the GitHub Shop!

I'm a newbie. What's in it for me?

If you need a push to start contributing, this is it! You not only build your profile, you earn points and you're contributing back.
